NHS dental treatment costs

You generally have to pay for NHS dental care and treatment. How much you pay depends on what you’re having done.

NHS dental care is free for children and for people on some benefits.

NHS dental charges
Band Treatment Cost
Band A Check-up, advice, polish, essential X-rays £22.70
Band B Fillings, root canal, removing teeth £62.10
Band C More complex treatments such as crowns and bridges £269.30

Find out more about dental costs

You may be able to get help with dental costs if you need it.

You should check what help you could get to pay for NHS costs. This is because you may be fined up to £100 plus the cost of your treatment if you claim for free treatment you’re not entitled to.
