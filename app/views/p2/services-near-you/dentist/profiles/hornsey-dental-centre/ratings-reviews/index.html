Friendly and helpful practice

★★★★★

by Maelan - Posted on 06 July 2019

I've been with this practice about 10 years. I'm disabled and everything is ground floor. The only issue is this weird dip just inside the door, so I leave my scooter outside. There is a roomy disabled toilet and the staff are helpful and considerate of my extra needs. I'm quite an anxious patient but my female dentist is very calm and understanding. She books appointments for me with extra time so I can take a short break if I get stressed. I would definitely recommend using this practice for NHS and private patients.
